Choosing Your Guitar

For Beginners:

Start with an acoustic or classical guitar to develop finger strength and basic techniques. Classical guitars with nylon strings are easier on the fingers, while steel-string acoustics offer more versatility.

For Rock & Blues:

Electric guitars are essential for these genres. Look for solid-body electrics with humbucker or single-coil pickups depending on your preferred sound.

For Classical Music:

Classical guitars with nylon strings and traditional construction are specifically designed for fingerstyle playing and classical repertoire.

For Band Playing:

Consider both electric guitar and bass guitar. The bass provides the rhythmic foundation while electric guitar handles melody and harmony.
